Cooking Time

Paneer Bhurji

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, crumbled
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 - 1 green chili, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste (optional, but good)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and green chili. Saute for 2-3 minutes until onion is translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste (if using) and saute for 30 seconds.
  4. Stir in chopped tomato, turmeric, and red chili powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes until tomatoes soften slightly.
  5. Add crumbled paneer, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring gently, until heated through.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
